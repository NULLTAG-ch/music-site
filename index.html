    Kino ohne Bilder. Filme entstehen im Kopf, nicht auf der Leinwand. Cinematic techno · cosmic frenchcore · German underground.

    NULLTAG is a one-person Swiss music label. Releases ship across five Schienen — release rails sharing a sound profile and a visual signature. Catalog numbers, BPM, key, and ISRC are treated as design, not metadata to hide.

    Brutalist verbal language, single TIEFROT mark per surface, JetBrains Mono everywhere. The label sees itself as a small operation that happens to spec releases like a research lab — physics-as-lyrics, BPM-as-tag, every track gets a style-block treated as a primary source.
